Isla Rose

August nineteenth 10pm

Isla Rose is a singer-songwriter from the UK with a voice that's as delicate as it is powerful. Her music blends elements of folk, indie, and pop to create a sound that's both timeless and contemporary. Isla's intimate and introspective lyrics draw from personal experiences, exploring themes of love, loss, and self-discovery. With influences ranging from Joni Mitchell to Bon Iver, Isla Rose has a unique and authentic sound that is captivating and inspiring. Her live performances are a must-see, as she effortlessly transitions from heart-wrenching ballads to upbeat sing-alongs that leave audiences feeling uplifted and empowered. Isla Rose is a rising star in the UK music scene, and it's only a matter of time before the rest of the world falls in love with her music.
