Echoes from the Edge

July Thirteenth 9pm

Echoes from the Edge is a four-piece progressive metal band from Seattle, Washington. The band is known for its intricate arrangements, epic song structures, and virtuosic instrumental performances. The band's songs are steeped in themes of mythology, spirituality, and the human experience, exploring dark and introspective subject matter with unflinching intensity. With influences ranging from Tool to Opeth, Echoes from the Edge has a sound that's both technical and emotionally powerful. Their live shows are a must-see for fans of progressive metal, with the band delivering a truly immersive and transformative experience that transports audiences to another world.
