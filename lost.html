The Lost Shadows

June Twenty-First 8pm

The Lost Shadows is a four-piece alternative rock band from New York City, known for their moody, introspective music and powerful live performances. The Lost Shadows' songs are filled with themes of heartbreak, loss, and self-discovery, exploring the depths of the human experience with unflinching honesty. With influences ranging from Radiohead to Florence + the Machine, The Lost Shadows have a sound that's both unique and familiar, drawing on the best elements of alternative rock and indie pop. Their live shows are a captivating and immersive experience, with the band's emotionally charged performances leaving audiences spellbound. .
