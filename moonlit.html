Moonlit Memories

June Second at 8pm

Moonlit Memories is a four-piece indie rock band from Portland, Oregon. Their dreamy, atmospheric soundscapes are characterized by jangly guitars, ethereal synths, and hauntingly beautiful vocal harmonies. Drawing inspiration from artists like The War on Drugs and Beach House, Moonlit Memories creates music that is both nostalgic and forward-thinking, blending classic indie rock and shoegaze influences with a modern sensibility. With their captivating live shows and introspective lyrics, Moonlit Memories has quickly become a rising star in the Pacific Northwest music scene, and they are poised to make a big impact in the indie rock world.
